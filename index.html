Welcome New EV owners! We hope your experience with your new car is great. Please join us at our monthly meetings to share your experience with your EV. Or if you are thinking of buying a new electric car and want to get some first hand experience, come and ask questions of current owners. We have plenty of enthusiasts in our group. To get the latest information on the Monthly Meeting schedule, Click Here!

Just a reminder that all of our meetings are free and open to the public. They are informative meetings about EVs and related technologies. Questions are welcome and encouraged. We meet jointly with the NTREG on the second Saturday of the month. At our meetings, we hope you learn a lot about EVs and how they can greatly impact our lives and our environment. There are probably EVs driving around you right now that you don’t even notice. Then again there are EVs out there that you can’t keep your eyes off of. We hope you will come to one of our meetings where we discuss EV and EV topics and also play with some of the EVs after the meetings. We always try to get a few EVs to every meeting to show off and have fun.

Contact NTEAA :
If you would like to contact NTEAA about meetings and/or events, please send an email to nteaa.org@gmail.com and one of the officers will respond to your email. If you would like to take part of our conversations and learn more about EVs, please join our Yahoo! Group. To join is free, just send in your request and you will get approved. We use the approval method to prevent spammers in our group, but you will be approved shortly after submitting your request.
